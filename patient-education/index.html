Patient Education

Orthopaedic medicine deals with the treatment and care of conditions and injuries related to bones, joints, ligaments, nerves and tendons. Dr. Myers is an American Board of Orthopaedic Surgery Certified Physician and treats a wide variety of orthopaedic and sports medicine conditions in patients of all ages.

This section has been created to educate visitors about some of the conditions and traumas that Dr. Myers sees on a regular basis and provides some of the treatment options available to our patients.
